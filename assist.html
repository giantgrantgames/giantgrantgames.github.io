Assisting Giant Grant Games

Giant Grant Games is a project ran by one person, however there are a limit to the things that I can do myself. Below is a list of work for Ascendancy to be contracted out to talented artists. Ascendancy is a Semi-Realistic fantasy setting with a predominately 10th-13th century Western European style.

Currently I am searching for artists capable of any of the following:

  • Pixel art sprite sheets
  • Pixel art tilesets
  • Character Portraits in a Visual Novel style
    •
  • Backgrounds in a Visual Novel Style
    •
  • Menu and UI design

Sprite Sheets:

  • Character sprites are 40x40px
  • Each sprite sheet involves 1 idle frame, cardinal direction movement animations, an ability use animation, and cardinal direction attack animations
  • High quality sprites use 5 frames per animation ~40 frame sheet
  • Average quality sprites use 3 frames per animation ~20 frame sheet
  • 10 High quality sprites and 15 average Quality sprite sheets required
  • Not a partial job, to insure artistic consistency
  • Time and quality permitting after completion, additional sprite animations and more sprite sheets may be negotiable at the same pay rate

Tilesets:

  • Tiles are 40x40px
  • Tiles are top-down RPG style tiles.
  • Split into two categories: Tiles and Doodads
  • Tile examples: Dirt, Water, Grass, Road and Stone Floor tiles
  • Doodad examples: Trees, Bushes, Rubble, Buildings (larger than 40x40), Fire
  • Potentially a partial job. Would prefer one artist for all tilesets, however a full commitment to either Tiles or Doodads may be acceptable
  • Time and quality permitting after completion, additional variations of tiles and doodads may be negotiable at the same pay rate

Character Portraits

  • Portraits built to fit 1920x1080 screens
  • ~20 portraits required
  • Character design is not finalized, assisting in fleshing out the details of character design is a core duty.
  • A great opportunity for an artist to communicate their style in Gaming Art.
    •

Backgrounds

  • Backgrounds are 1920x1080 or upscaled 1280x720
  • Initially asking for seven (7) Backgrounds
  • Not strict in design, open to artist's interpretation
  • Not a partial job, payment is delivered upon completion of all 7 backgrounds
  • Time and quality permitting after completion, additional backgrounds may be negotiable at the same pay rate

Menu and UI Design

  • Create a cohesive consistent interface for Ascendancy
  • Design Buttons, Cursors and Menus
  • Mostly a design job, as menus and buttons tend to be copies

If you are interested or want further information, email jobs@giantgrantgames.com with the subject line "Jobs"
